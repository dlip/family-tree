Narrative

THE BONDI TRAGEDY. Tho man who was found shot dead at Bondi on Thursday ,was identified in the afternoon as John Aceglav, aged 21, who lived with his father at The Fisheries, 104 Hunter-street, Sydney. He used to sleep at his father's stable in Junction-street, Woolloomooloo. The woman, who had also been shot, was identified as Annie Sutherland, a married woman, aged 24, who has been living apart from her husband in Goulburn-street, Sydney. They were last noticed alive about 8 p.m. on Wednesday, when they called on a married sister of the deceased woman in Stanley-street, Woolloomooloo. They left the house together.
