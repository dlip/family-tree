Obituary Mrs Sarah Ann Marr

 

An old and respected resident of Orange, Mrs Sarah Ann Marr, passed away at her residence, 189 Byng Street, on Saturday, after a lifetime devoted to her family. She was 87 years of age. Mrs Marr was born at Orange and for many years together with the members of her family, lived at the Byng Street railway crossing. Her husband, who pre-deceased her many years, was a railway employee. A family of five survive, viz Mrs B Pendall (of Orange), Mrs W Spicer (Euchareena), Mrs GH Brown, Mrs NH Brown, Mrs H Titheradge (all of Orange). There are 18 grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren. Mrs Powter (Parkes) is a sister. Mrs Marr's seven sons predeceased her, the last one, Harold, just a little more than a year ago. Her funeral was very largely attended and left her late residence on Sunday afternoon for the Methodist Church, where a service was held, and then to the Methodist portion of the Orange cemetery. Rev DF Almond officiated and Mr McGrath had charge of the arrangements.
